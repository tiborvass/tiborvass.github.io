Why packathon.org?

Hackathons are the way for hackers to share their vision of the world by coding, designing and celebrating innovation and community during 24 to 48h. More and more hackathons are organized in the world every month and it’s a happy fact.

But today, the Hackathons are going on the wrong side of the force for mainly 2 reasons :

- it’s time consuming and quite difficult to organize such event ! Find a room, sponsors, contact developers, make marketing material. This is not what you want to do and lots of hackers don’t even try to make one.

- because of this difficulty to organize Hackathons, too much hackathons become business-oriented and won by powerpoint driven mockups or sponsors oriented applications.

Too often organizers have forgotten than Hackathons has to be fun driven more than prize driven.

This is the main reason why we made a Hackathon-in-a-box, a pack of all you need to organize a hackathon on the fly, everywhere and create amazing apps with serendipity and genius with your developer community and your hacker spirit.

Because Hack is art!
